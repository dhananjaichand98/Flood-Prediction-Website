About TechBusters

We are the TechBusters, and we aspire to solve real-life problems. In a flood-ridden country like India, we believe that we have a found a way to save lots of lives and millions in property damage every year when disaster strikes. Using our combined skills of machine learning, web development and designing, we have come up with a model that can help us predict future floods using a dataset of the history of floods throughout the country. To make it available to the public, we have also developed a state-of-the-art website that acts a frontend. We are just everyday college students with a dream to make it big in the world, and believe that this will push us closer toward it.
